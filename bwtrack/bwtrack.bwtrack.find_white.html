bwtrack.bwtrack.find_white¶

bwtrack.bwtrack.find_white(img, size=7, mask_size=None, mask_pattern='dw', thres=None, std_thres=None, plot_hist=False)¶

Find “white” particles in Qiaoge’s images of particles at water-oil interface.

Parameters

  • img – input image

  • size – particle size to look for (px)

  • mask_size – mask (template) image size (px). By default, mask_size is set to size+2. Making mask slightly larger can help making the correlation map sharper, sometimes.

  • mask_pattern – choose from “mh”, “gs” and “dw”, which stands for mexican hat, gaussian and double well, respectively.

  • thres – mean intensity threshold, meant to discern white particles from black particles.

  • std_thres – standard deviation threshold, meant to discern white particles from black particles.

Plot_hist

if True, plot mean intensity and standard deviation histogram. This can help you to determine good threshold values. Set to False when doing batch tracking (default).

Return particles

a list of particle coordinates detected.

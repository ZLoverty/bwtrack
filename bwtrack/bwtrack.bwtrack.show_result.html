bwtrack.bwtrack.show_result¶

bwtrack.bwtrack.show_result(img, particles, size=7, ROI=None)¶

Convenient function to plot particles on top of the raw image.

Parameters

  • img – raw image

  • particles – result of find_black() or find_white

  • size – particle size (px)

  • ROI – region of interest, defined as (left, right, bottom, top)

Returns

fig, ax, matplotlib handles for further adjustment.
